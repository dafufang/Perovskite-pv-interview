## Candidate answer patterns

### Project experience

Chinese:

> 这个项目的目标是解决 X 问题。我负责 Y 部分，主要方法是 Z。我们通过 A/B 对照和 N 个器件统计验证了结果，核心指标从 M 提升到 N。表征上，PL/TRPL/EQE/XRD 等结果支持了我们的机制判断。我的个人贡献主要是……局限性是……下一步我会……

English:

> The goal of this project was to address X. My responsibility was Y, and the main approach was Z. We validated the result through A/B controls and statistics from N devices. The key metric improved from M to N. Characterization such as PL, TRPL, EQE, or XRD supported our proposed mechanism. My personal contribution was... The limitation was... The next step would be...

### Failure case

Chinese:

> 一开始我们观察到 X 异常。我把可能原因分成材料、界面、工艺和测量四类，然后通过 Y 实验逐步排除。最后发现主要问题是 Z。这个经历让我意识到……

English:

> Initially, we observed X. I grouped the possible causes into materials, interfaces, process, and measurement artifacts, then ruled them out using Y experiments. The main issue turned out to be Z. The key lesson was...

### Personal contribution

Chinese:

> 这个结果是团队合作完成的。我个人主要负责 A、B、C；同事负责 D；导师/负责人参与了 E 决策。因此我能详细解释 A、B、C 的实验设计、数据和失败复盘。

English:

> This was a team effort. My personal ownership was A, B, and C. My teammates handled D, and my supervisor contributed to E. Therefore, I can explain the experimental design, data, and troubleshooting behind A, B, and C in detail.

## Interviewer phrasing

- “Can you separate your personal contribution from the team's contribution?”
- “What was the device area, and was the result stabilized or scan-based?”
- “How many devices were included in the statistics?”
- “What evidence supports the mechanism rather than only the performance improvement?”
- “Which subcell limited the tandem current, and how did you verify that?”
- “What would fail first if this process were scaled to module size?”
