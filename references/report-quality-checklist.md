# Report Quality Checklist

Use this checklist before finalizing a PDF report or report-ready Markdown output.

## Template selection

- Confirm the report type: candidate preparation, interviewer candidate evaluation, or mock interview feedback.
- Use the matching framework in `references/pdf-report-template.md`.
- Do not merge templates unless the user explicitly wants multiple outputs.

## Required content checks

- Role is explicit: candidate or interviewer/hiring side.
- Target role and seniority are stated or assumptions are labeled.
- Technical focus is stated: single-junction, tandem, stability, encapsulation, scale-up, equipment, materials, or leadership.
- User-provided facts are separated from AI analysis.
- Missing or ambiguous items are listed as Items to verify.
- Resume claims are not treated as validated achievements unless evidence is provided.
- Perovskite-specific checks are included where relevant: efficiency protocol, device area, statistics, steady-state output, EQE consistency, stability protocol, scale-up, encapsulation, tandem current matching, personal contribution.
- Scorecard uses calibrated scoring when a hiring judgment is requested.
- Final recommendation includes confidence level and next-step questions.

## PDF rendering checks

- Headings are hierarchical and readable.
- Tables do not overflow pages when possible.
- Chinese characters render correctly.
- English terms and chemical/technical abbreviations remain readable.
- Page breaks do not split critical tables awkwardly when avoidable.
- Filename matches the report type or user request.

## Safety and integrity checks

- Do not fabricate candidate facts, publications, certified efficiencies, patents, device areas, or stability results.
- Do not coach the candidate to misrepresent experience.
- Use uncertainty labels for incomplete resume extraction or poor-quality uploaded files.
